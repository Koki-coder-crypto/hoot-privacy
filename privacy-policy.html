ScanVolt AI Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026 | Last Updated: May 25, 2026

Short version: ScanVolt AI processes QR content on-device first. URL safety checks use anonymized hashes — we never log the full URLs you scan. No account is required. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

ScanVolt AI ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile application developed and operated by Koki (contact: kouki_1203@icloud.com). ScanVolt AI is available on the Apple App Store.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • None required. ScanVolt AI does not require account registration. You can use all core scanning features without providing any personal information.
  • If you contact support, we collect your email address and the content of your message solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Scan data (on-device only): QR code content is decoded on your device. The raw content is stored locally in your scan history and never uploaded to our servers.
  • URL safety hashes: For AI safety analysis, we extract the destination URL from a QR code, compute a one-way SHA-256 hash of that URL, and send only the hash to our analysis service. The original URL is never transmitted.
  • Usage analytics (anonymous): We collect non-identifiable crash reports and aggregate feature usage data (e.g., "X scans performed today") via on-device aggregation. This data cannot be linked to you.
  • Purchase records: In-App Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We receive only a receipt token; no payment card data is stored by ScanVolt AI.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Full URLs of QR codes you scan
  • Images or photos you use to scan QR codes
  • Your location
  • Your name, email, or any identifier (unless you contact support)
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Safety Analysis: Anonymized URL hashes are sent to our backend service, which queries the Anthropic Claude API to assess threat risk. The response (Safe / Caution / Danger + explanation) is returned to your device. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing: anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Service improvement: Aggregate, non-identifiable crash and usage data helps us fix bugs and improve the app.
  • Support: Email addresses provided when contacting support are used only to respond to your request and are deleted after 90 days.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Anonymized URL hashes for safety analysis only. See Anthropic's DPA and privacy policy for their obligations.
  • Apple: Purchase transactions and crash symbolication handled by Apple's platform.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our legal rights, we may disclose information. We will notify you if legally permitted to do so.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • On-device: Scan history is stored on your device (and iCloud, if sync is enabled for Pro users) and is retained according to your plan (7 days free, 1 year Pro). You can delete your history at any time from the app.
  • Our servers: We do not retain URL hashes after returning the safety analysis result. Response time is typically under 2 seconds; no log of the hash is written.
  • iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync (Pro feature), your scan history is stored in your private iCloud container. Apple's iCloud privacy terms apply. We cannot access your iCloud data.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal data and no account is required, most rights are exercised on-device:

  • Access & deletion: Clear your scan history in Settings → History → Clear All.
  • Revoke camera access: iOS Settings → ScanVolt AI → Camera → Off.
  • Support data: Email kouki_1203@icloud.com to request deletion of any support correspondence.

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

7. Children's Privacy

ScanVolt AI is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us immediately at kouki_1203@icloud.com.

8. Security

Scan history is stored using iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest). Network requests use TLS 1.3. URL hashes are computed locally before transmission and are not reversible to the original URL.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as ScanVolt AI evolves. Material changes will be announced in the App Store release notes and within the app. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
